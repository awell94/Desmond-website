Funk Force

A pastiche of Japanese anime and blaxploitation, Funk Force is a satirical short film that follows the mission of a dysfunktional team of young black superheroes as they struggle against their most daunting foe to date: A Hip Hop sensation hell bent on hypnotizing the community the heroes protect.
