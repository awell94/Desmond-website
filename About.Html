Bio

A radical writer and director originally from New York invested in crafting African-centered stories in the action and horror genres.

While based in Los Angeles, Desmond has participated in the 2019 Academy Gold program, the 2021 Facebook Seen Program and the 2022 Deutsche Bank Frieze Los Angeles Film Fellowship.

His short films have garnered recognition across several film festivals. His horror film “Wolves” was lauded Best Low Budget Short at the Long Island Scared for Your LIIFE Film Festival and his action short“Beaten But Not Broken” was an awardwinner at the Black Truth Film Festival. Desmond also acts and performs poetry.

Accolades

Master degree in flim making

Master degree Alum of the Academy Gold 2019 program, a 2020 Academy certified story analyst, 2020 HPA Young Entertainment Professional, 2020 Charley Film Grant Recipient, a 2021 Facebook Seen Fellow, and a 2022 Deutsche Bank Frieze LA Film Fellow.


Director Statement

Influenced heavily by the filmmakers Jordan Peele, Terrance Nance, and the aesthetic of Japanese action anime, Desmond boasts a dynamic cinematic style that he brings to his action and horror projects.

With his films, Desmond hopes to uplift African people and inspire them to be critical of systemic institutions and of the nuances of African people, in order to bring about unity within the African Diaspora.
